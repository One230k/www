Legislation by The People, a distributed marketplace of ideas

Power

Take the power to advance ideas away from the political few and vest it in a digital marketplace where the best ideas in full public view can rise to the top.

Distributed ledgers (aka blockchains), in trust-less systems from currency to contracts, are replacing bulky governmental dinosaurs. It can keep track of countless variations in ideas, organized by subject, and advance those with the necessary virutal political currency.

Tools

Supporting the tens of thousands of citizen legislators are the moble and desktop tools we'd expect.

  • Find out what the law is now.
  • Engage with others about what the law should be.
  • Track subjects and popularity with analytics.
  • Earn virtual political currency and invest in the ideas you want advanced.
